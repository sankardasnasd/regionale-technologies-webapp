Our Story

25 Years of Building What Matters

Regional Technologies was founded in 1999 with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry-ready skills. Over two and a half decades, that mission has expanded into something larger — a full-service software company that designs, engineers, and deploys production software for clients across healthcare, logistics, e-commerce, education, and fintech.

Today we operate as both a software development partner and a professional training institute. Our engineers work on live client projects while our educators train the next generation of developers with hands-on, real-world curricula. Every training program is shaped by what we actually build in production — which means our graduates are job-ready from day one.

We're based in Kozhikode, Kerala, and serve clients nationally and internationally. Whether you're a startup looking to ship your first product, an enterprise modernizing legacy systems, or a fresh graduate looking to break into tech — Regional Technologies is built to meet you where you are and take you further.
